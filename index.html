Assymandias

A story as old as time, a great leader and dictator, known across multiple universes for his cruelty, discovers he controls a power so ultimate.



Immediately, he commands a servant so faithful to come to his aid, but as always, he had cried wolf and the servant suffers at his hand.



As is common, he pushes the power too far, he flies too close to the sun, and turns this ability into a weapon to rival all which came before.
